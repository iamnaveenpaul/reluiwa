WHY RELATAS

Making sales teams successful. #DoSales.

The biggest challenge that most CEO, CRO, VP Sales, Sales Reps face today is unpredictable sales forecasting. This happens mainly due to no-data or worse, bad data in the CRM. Commit call are more of a status call, Sales reps hate entering data into CRMs leading to limited visibility for management and limited to no insights for sales reps.

With No-Data-Entry CRM, Relatas pulls in authentic data. We apply Artificial Intelligence to solve one of the biggest challenge - Sales Forecasting.

Relatas is used by professionals from Enterprises, mid-sized companies to startups.

Relatas is built by sales professionals for sales professionals.

We know sales and are obsessed with making the sales professionals more successful by applying Artificial Intelligence to solve their problems. With so much information overload, Relatas makes sense of the data thats readily available and uses cognitive technology to make sense of that data to helps sales professionals sell better and faster.

WHERE DID IT START

Relatas was founded by Sudip, who led teams for 20 years across 4 continents. Relatas is Sudip's life-story. Having experienced, first hand, the challenges of sales forecast where his sales team won't enter details in their existing CRM's thus leading to challenges in Sales Forecasting. Thus started the journey to build one of its kind No-Data-Entry CRM focusing on Sales Forecasting. Along the way, Sudip was joined by some of the most exciting minds in the industry. Robby who was leading Solution Consulting for Amazon in India and London, joined hands with Sudip to build the best AI solution in sales.

Since its founding in 2015, Relatas has been one of the most-impactful enterprise SaaS companies and has office in Fremont, USA and development team in Bangalore, India. We pride ourselves in having a culture that blends fun and learning with impactful work. And we firmly believe that customer relationship starts with happy team. Passionate, Ownership driven teams, when given the freedom to execute bedded in a culture of learning, create impact and change the world for the better.
