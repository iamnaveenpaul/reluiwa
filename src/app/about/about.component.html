Relatas is built by sales professionals for sales professionals. The biggest challenge that most CEO, CRO, VP Sales, Sales Reps face today are unpredictable sales forecasting, bad data in the CRM, commit call are more of a status call, Sales reps hate entering data into CRMs leading to limited visibility for management and limited to no insights for sales reps. We apply Artificial Intelligence to solve some of the biggest challenges in Sales. We know sales and are obsessed with making the sales professionals more successful by applying Artificial Intelligence to solve their problems.
