COMPANY

Relatas is an award winning Sales Intelligence product.

Relatas is built by sales professionals for sales professionals. The biggest challenge that most CEO, CRO, VP Sales, Sales Reps face today are unpredictable forecasting, bad data in the CRM, commit call are more of a status call, Sales reps hate entering data into CRMs leading to limited visibility for management and limited to no insights for sales reps. We apply Artificial Intelligence to solve some of the biggest challenges in Sales. We know sales and are obsessed with making the sales professionals more successful by applying Artificial Intelligence to solve their problems. With so much information overload, Relatas makes sense of the data thats readily available and uses cognitive technology to make sense of that data to helps sales professionals sell better and faster.

Mission: Make sales professionals successful by transforming the way they view data, sell, make decisions, and grow.

Here is how we are making sales easier for Sales Reps and give visibility with authentic data to CEO/ CRO/ VP Sales and Sales Reps.

  • CEO/ CRO/ VP Sales - As the CEO/ CRO/ VP Sales, you have to make sure that the commitments you have made to the markets/ investors are met. Maximise and grow your revenue while delivering best customer experience to your customers.
  • Sales Manager Success - As a sales leader, spot pipeline risk & drive more revenue by analysing right and authentic data. Relatas helps you ask the right questions and provides you the data you need. Move from 1-0-1 weekly status calls (commit calls) to 1-0-1 strategy calls. Make your commit calls more effective.
  • Sales Rep Success - Be more productive & close deals faster by automating data entry into CRM. Relatas Artificial Intelligence Engine understands how you close deals and predicts how likely are you to meet your targets and suggests ways to meet your numbers.
